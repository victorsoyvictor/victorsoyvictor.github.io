Víctor-さん is an International Ph.D. in Mathematics cum laude, currently working for Siemes-Gamesa as a Senior Expert Engineer.

I am a Computer Scientist, Master in Systems and Control Engineering and received the International Ph.D. in Mathematics cum laude in Polytechnic University of Valencia, Instituto de Matemática Multidisciplinar.

My research interest includes mathematical models, machine learning, deep learning and computer vision.

My dissertation's field is 'Papillomavirus transmission dynamics on lifetime sexual partners networks' using random networks and PSO algorithm.

I also work for a private company called Siemes-Gamesa in Spain, where I develop internal applications for mathematicians and physicists.
